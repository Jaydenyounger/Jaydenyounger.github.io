---
I am an aspiring creative leader driven to make a meaningful impact in the gaming industry. I create games that give players a sense of control and power, while placing them in worlds where that control is constantly challenged. This contrast is what motivates me as a designer. I believe games, like any form of entertainment, should move people pushing them to feel, reflect, and ultimately find the courage to stand on their own and face the world head-on.
---

My design philosophy begins with intention. When I start a project, I do not just define a genre I define the experience. I focus on what I want the player to feel, whether that is tension, empowerment, or perseverance. From there, I build a strong foundation, ensuring that every system and mechanic supports that vision. 
---

Using my background in game design, I prioritize structure and clarity early in development, creating a solid framework that allows creativity to grow without losing direction. I work with industry-standard tools such as Unreal Engine, Substance Painter, ZBrush, and Maya, combining my skills in design, programming, and art to bring ideas to life. I approach learning by doing, taking on challenges, experimenting with systems, and continuously refining my craft through iteration.
Beyond design, I am a hands-on creator who thrives in collaborative environments. I do not stand on the sidelines; I actively engage with my team, learning and growing alongside them. This mindset allows me to contribute across multiple disciplines while staying focused on the larger creative vision.
---

My goal is to empower players through meaningful experiences that resonate beyond the screen. By placing players in difficult worlds and giving them the tools to overcome them, I hope to inspire confidence, resilience, and the drive to take on challenges in their own lives.
---
